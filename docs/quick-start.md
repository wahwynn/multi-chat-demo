# Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with Multi-Chat Demo in minutes!

## First Steps

### 1. Create Your Account

1. Open the application in your browser
2. Click "Sign up" if you see the sign-in form
3. Enter:
   - A username
   - Your email address
   - A password (minimum 6 characters)
   - Confirm your password
4. Click "Create account"

![Sign up](screenshots/02-sign-up.png)

### 2. Create Your First Conversation

1. In the left sidebar, select one or more AI models from the dropdown
2. Click the "New Chat" button
3. Your new conversation appears in the list

![New conversation](screenshots/04-new-conversation.png)

### 3. Start Chatting

1. Type your message in the text box at the bottom
2. Press Enter or click "Send"
3. Watch as AI models respond!

![Chat interface](screenshots/09-chat-responses.png)

## Pro Tips

- **Compare Models**: Select multiple models to see different responses side-by-side
- **Organize**: Double-click conversation titles to rename them
- **Customize**: Click your avatar to edit your profile and change settings
